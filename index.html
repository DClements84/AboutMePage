About Me

Danielle Clements has been a stay-at-home-mom for the past six years, and is now considering her eventual return to the workforce. In the fall of 2020, Danielle completed her B.A. from Memorial University, but her prior work experience is mainly in office administration. Danielle enjoys activities such as running and biking, reading and gaming, and baking and cooking. She lives in Clarenville, NL, with her family.
